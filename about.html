About Me

Started career with sales in the B2B software industry; learning & experiencing hands-on the fundamentals of business, i.e. client, partner & supply-chain management. Progressed next to marketing, managing diverse international brands and handling a wide range of roles, from delivering the front-end results that are visible to the public, to the back-end workings that make things happen.

My experiences in different industries working in a retailer & distributor of international brands (B2C), a public-listed financial MNC (B2B & B2C) and a global maritime software company (B2B) allowed me to develop a diverse and holistic understanding in managing the intricacies of marketing and the many stakeholders involved. This would not be possible without the knowledge and opportunities imparted by the leaders whom I have worked with and learned from.

Currently I'm managing the worldwide marketing for BASS Software, a leading global provider of Fleet Management Software for the maritime industry with 5 offices worldwide. More than 100 customers, including many of the world’s leading shipping companies and offshore service providers, run fully-integrated BASSnet™ Fleet Management Systems on more than 2,000 vessels around the world.
